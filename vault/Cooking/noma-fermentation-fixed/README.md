# The Noma Guide to Fermentation

> *"Our story with fermentation is a story of accidents."* — René Redzepi

This is a structured course based on **The Noma Guide to Fermentation** by René Redzepi and David Zilber.

## Course Overview

This course covers the fermentation techniques developed and used at Noma, one of the world's most innovative restaurants.

## What You'll Learn

- **Lacto-fermentation** — Transform fruits and vegetables
- **Kombucha** — Brew and flavor fermented tea
- **Vinegar** — Make vinegars from various sources
- **Koji** — Cultivate Aspergillus oryzae
- **Misos and Peaso** — Create fermented pastes
- **Shoyu** — Brew soy sauce
- **Garum** — Make ancient Roman-style sauces
- **Black Fruits & Vegetables** — Master Maillard reactions

*Note: This course is for educational purposes. Always follow proper food safety guidelines when fermenting.*
